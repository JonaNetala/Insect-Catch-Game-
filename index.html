How to Play

Catch Mode

Click on insects to catch them before they multiply out of control!

  • Each insect caught gives you points based on its type
  • The game gets harder as you level up
  • Higher levels give more points per catch

Battle Mode

Select your insect and battle against enemy insects!

  • Each insect has different Power and Speed stats
  • Take turns attacking, defending, or using special moves
  • Win battles to earn bonus points
